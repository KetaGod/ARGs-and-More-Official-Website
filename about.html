About The Creator

I originally created this show just for myself as I'm always listening to podcasts while working since, to me at least,

it seems to make the time go by quicker. So instead of just posting these for myself, I wanted to post them for more people

that may share a similar mindset as I have! In the end, this show was created both for people who are working or are still in school

and want something to listen to whilst they're doing their own schoolwork. I hope you enjoy whats on this page and if you have any

any requests, concerns, or comments feel free to reach out to me! I will get back to you as soon as possible! -Keta
